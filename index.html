Why Ethereum Needs Layer 2

Ethereum is secure and decentralized, but it can become expensive and slow during high usage. Layer 2 networks process transactions more cheaply and settle results back to Ethereum.

Arbitrum is a Layer 2 solution that helps Ethereum apps become faster and cheaper while keeping Ethereum security.

Benefit: users can use DeFi, games, and NFT apps with lower gas fees than Ethereum mainnet.
