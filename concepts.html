Core Web3 Concepts

Simple comparison cards for important Web3 fundamentals.

Web2 vs Web3

Web2: Companies control platforms and store user data on private servers.

Web3: Users connect with wallets and can own assets, tokens, and identity.

Ethereum vs Bitcoin

Bitcoin: Designed mainly as decentralized digital money.

Ethereum: A programmable blockchain for smart contracts, dApps, DeFi, and NFTs.

Public Key vs Private Key

Public key: Your visible address for receiving funds.

Private key: Your secret proof of ownership. Never share it.

Blockchain vs Database

Database: Usually controlled by one organization.

Blockchain: Shared by many computers and difficult to secretly change.
